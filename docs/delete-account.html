⚠ ACCOUNT DELETION

Delete Your Account

You can request full deletion of your Velox account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what gets deleted, what is kept, and how to submit your request.

How to request deletion

1
Send an email to our support address Write to velox-app@zohomail.eu from the email address linked to your Velox account.
2
Use the subject line: "Account deletion request" Include your registered email address in the body of the message so we can identify your account.
3
We will process your request within 30 days You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

What data is deleted

Upon confirmed deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (email and hashed password)
  • Your profile — username, country, speed unit, vehicle information
  • All trip records — routes, speeds, scores, and extended driving metrics
  • Weekly insights associated with your account

What data may be retained

The following may be retained for a limited period after deletion:

  • Subscription records — Google Play and RevenueCat retain purchase receipts for legal and financial compliance. These records do not contain personal driving data and are governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Anonymised analytics — if any aggregated, non-identifiable data was previously included in statistical summaries, it cannot be retroactively removed as it contains no personal identifiers.

⚠ Deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your trip history, driving scores, and all associated data cannot be recovered. Make sure to export anything you want to keep before requesting deletion.

Active subscriptions

Deleting your Velox account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. To avoid future charges, cancel your subscription separately through Google Play → Subscriptions before or after submitting your deletion request.
